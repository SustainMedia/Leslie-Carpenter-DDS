What is TMJ and Why Does it Hurt?

Your jaw has a special joint (called the temporomandibular joint or TMJ) that works like a sliding hinge. When this joint isn't working right, you can feel:

  • Painful clicking or popping when you open your mouth
  • Jaw that gets stuck open or closed
  • Headaches, especially in the morning
  • Face, neck, or ear pain
  • Trouble chewing or yawning

Many Pasadena residents don't realize these problems come from their jaw joint. Dr. Carpenter's experienced eye can spot the signs and help you find real relief.
