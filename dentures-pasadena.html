Adjusting to Your New Dentures:

Getting used to dentures takes some time. This is normal. At first, you might notice:

  • The dentures feel big in your mouth
  • You produce more saliva than usual
  • Some foods are harder to eat
  • Speaking certain words takes practice

Don't worry! These issues improve as you adjust. We give you detailed care instructions and tips to help during this transition period. Most patients fully adjust within a few weeks.

Caring for Your Dentures:

Taking good care of your dentures helps them last longer and keeps your mouth healthy. We teach you how to:

  • Clean your dentures properly each day
  • Store them safely when not wearing them
  • Check for signs of wear or damage
  • Know when adjustments are needed
