Our Cosmetic Dentistry Process

When you visit us for cosmetic dentistry, we'll start with a smile consultation. We'll talk about what you like and don't like about your smile. We may take photos and measurements of your teeth to help plan your treatment.

Next, we'll explain your options and help you choose the treatments that fit your goals and budget. Some treatments can be completed in just one visit, while others might take a few appointments to finish.

Before any permanent changes, we'll often show you what your new smile will look like. This might be through digital images or temporary materials on your teeth.

Will Insurance Cover Cosmetic Dentistry?

Some cosmetic treatments may be partially covered by dental insurance, especially if they also improve the function of your teeth. For example, replacing old metal fillings with tooth-colored ones might be covered. We'll help you understand your coverage and offer payment options for any out-of-pocket costs.

Is Cosmetic Dentistry Right for You?

If you feel embarrassed about your smile or find yourself hiding your teeth, cosmetic dentistry could be a great choice. Even small changes can make a big difference in how you look and feel.

Before cosmetic treatment, we'll make sure your teeth and gums are healthy. If you have cavities or gum disease, we'll need to treat those issues first to ensure your new smile will last.
