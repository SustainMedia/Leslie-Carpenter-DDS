What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots made of titanium that are placed in your jawbone. They provide a strong foundation for fixed or removable replacement teeth. Here's what makes them special:

  • They become part of your jaw through a process called osseointegration
  • They don't affect neighboring teeth like bridges do
  • They help keep your jawbone strong and healthy
  • They function just like natural teeth for eating and speaking
  • They can last a lifetime with proper care

Am I a Good Candidate for Dental Implants?

Most adults with missing teeth can get dental implants. During your consultation, we'll check:

  • If you have enough jawbone to support the implant
  • The health of your gums
  • Your overall health
  • If you smoke (smoking can affect implant success)

Even if you've been told in the past that you can't get implants, modern techniques might make it possible now. We also offer bone grafting if you need more jawbone support.

Caring for Your Dental Implants:

Taking care of dental implants is simple:

  • Brush twice a day just like natural teeth
  • Floss daily around the implant
  • Visit us for regular check-ups
  • Avoid chewing very hard items like ice or hard candy

With proper care, your implants can last a lifetime!
