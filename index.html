Meet Dr. Carpenter

Dr. Leslie M.H. Carpenter combines decades of experience with a heartfelt commitment to her patients.

Over 35 Years of experience

Dr. Leslie Carpenter has served families and individuals in her practice as a general dentist. Located near the Playhouse district of downtown Pasadena, located primarily.

Dr. Carpenter has been a resident of the Foothill communities for most of her life, with the exception being her years in San Francisco as a student at University of the Pacific Dugoni School of Dentistry.

Dr. Carpenter is continually active in local and national study groups and continuing education with an emphasis on occlusal “bite” evaluation, comprehensive and esthetic restoration, and maintenance necessary for comfort and stability in all ages.

She offers a great variety of procedures to this goal including digital radiographs, skillful periodontal care by her experienced hygienist staff, tooth colored and gold restorations, occlusal guards, and implant-supported rehabilitation.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing. Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Please feel free to chat our friendly team.

Do you accept dental insurance?

We accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team can help you understand your coverage and assist with filing claims to make your visit as smooth as possible.

Do you offer cosmetic dentistry services?

Yes! We provide a variety of cosmetic services including teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers to enhance the appearance of your smile.

How often should I get a checkup?

We recommend a visit every 6 months for regular cleanings and check-ups to keep your teeth healthy.

What age should children start seeing a dentist?

We recommend children see a dentist by their first birthday or when their first tooth appears, whichever comes first.
