What Happens During Your First Appointment?

Your visit includes several simple steps to get to know your dental health:

  1. A friendly welcome from our Pasadena team
  2. A conversation about your dental history and concerns
  3. A gentle but thorough exam of your teeth and gums
  4. Digital x-rays to see what's happening below the surface
  5. An oral cancer screening for your safety
  6. A check of your gum health
  7. Time to discuss what we found and answer your questions

No rushing, no pressure - just caring attention from Dr. Carpenter and our team.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing. Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Please feel free to chat our friendly team.

Do you accept dental insurance?

We accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team can help you understand your coverage and assist with filing claims to make your visit as smooth as possible.

Do you offer cosmetic dentistry services?

Yes! We provide a variety of cosmetic services including teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers to enhance the appearance of your smile.

How often should I get a checkup?

We recommend a visit every 6 months for regular cleanings and check-ups to keep your teeth healthy.

What age should children start seeing a dentist?

We recommend children see a dentist by their first birthday or when their first tooth appears, whichever comes first.
