Stay Ahead with Preventive Dental Exams

Your smile is one of your most valuable assets—and routine dental exams are the key to keeping it healthy and bright. At Leslie Carpenter Dentistry, we believe in the power of prevention.

Our Comprehensive Exam Process

Every routine exam at Leslie Carpenter Dentistry is designed to give you a complete picture of your oral health. Here’s what you can expect:

Thorough Cleaning

Our skilled hygienists use advanced tools to remove plaque and tartar buildup, leaving your teeth feeling fresh and polished.

Oral Cancer Screening

Early detection saves lives. We perform a meticulous screening to check for signs of oral cancer, ensuring peace of mind.

Digital X-Rays

High-resolution images allow us to spot hidden issues like cavities, impacted teeth, or bone loss that aren’t visible to the naked eye.

Gum Health Evaluation

We measure gum pockets and check for signs of gingivitis or periodontal disease, ensuring your gums stay healthy and strong.

Why Choose Routine Exams at Leslie Carpenter Dentistry

Routine exams are more than just a checkup—they’re an investment in your long-term health and confidence. Here’s what you’ll gain:

Early Detection

Catching problems early means simpler, less invasive treatments—and significant cost savings.

Preventive Care

Regular cleanings and exams help prevent cavities, gum disease, and other oral health issues before they start.

Improved Overall Health

Oral health is closely linked to systemic conditions like heart disease and diabetes. By taking care of your smile, you’re protecting your whole body.

Confidence Boost

Leave every appointment with a cleaner, brighter smile and the confidence that comes with knowing your oral health is in great shape.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing. Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Please feel free to chat our friendly team.

Do you accept dental insurance?

We accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team can help you understand your coverage and assist with filing claims to make your visit as smooth as possible.

Do you offer cosmetic dentistry services?

Yes! We provide a variety of cosmetic services including teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers to enhance the appearance of your smile.

How often should I get a checkup?

We recommend a visit every 6 months for regular cleanings and check-ups to keep your teeth healthy.

What age should children start seeing a dentist?

We recommend children see a dentist by their first birthday or when their first tooth appears, whichever comes first.
